Biography

Assistant professor of statistics at the School of Statistics, University of Costa Rica. She is interested in developing novel statistical methods to address scientific questions related to the environment. Her applications areas include climate, weather forecast and veterinary.

Marcela is commited to practice open science and she believes in mentoring young scientists. Her volunteering activities include being a co-chair of CODATA-RDA Schools of Data Science, organizing the R-ladies chapter in San José and recently she joined the organizing commitee of ConectaR2019 the first R Users Conference in Central America
